Client Onboarding Guide — Share this with your client before or during your onboarding call

Welcome to Lightning Ledgerz

This guide walks through the exact steps to get fully onboarded. It takes about 15–20 minutes and sets up everything needed to deliver your FP&A services. Follow the steps in order — each one unlocks the next.

Website: lightningledgerz.com  |  Email: zprizant@lightningledgerz.com  |  Phone: (702) 550-2513

1

Create Your Account

CLIENT ACTION
Client does this

Go to lightningledgerz.com and click "Get Started" in the top navigation. You'll be taken to the portal section where you can register.

  • Enter your first name, last name, company name, email, and phone
  • Create a secure password
  • Agree to the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
  • Click "Get Started" — your free 30-day trial starts immediately
Tip for Zac: If the client is on the call with you, you can navigate to the pricing section first and walk them through the "Find Your Plan" quiz together — it recommends the right package based on their needs.
2

Sign the Client Services Agreement

REQUIRED — CLIENT ACTION
Client does this

Immediately after logging in for the first time, a Client Services Agreement popup will appear. This is required before accessing the portal.

  • Enter your company name and authorized representative name exactly as they should appear on the agreement
  • Click "Generate My Agreement" to view the full agreement
  • Read through all sections (especially Sections 9 and 6 — confidentiality and limitation of liability)
  • Check all four acceptance boxes
  • Click "I Accept This Agreement — Enter Portal"
Important: Double-check company name spelling before accepting. The agreement is legally binding. If there's an error, contact zprizant@lightningledgerz.com for an amendment.
3

Upload Your Company Logo

OPTIONAL — PERSONALIZES YOUR PORTAL
Client does this

In the portal dashboard, find Step 1: Company Logo. Click the logo box or the "Upload Logo" button to upload your company's logo.

  • Accepted formats: PNG, JPG, SVG, WebP
  • This personalizes your dashboard and appears on your reports
4

Connect Your Accounting Platforms

CRITICAL — ENABLES SERVICE DELIVERY
Client does this

In Step 2: Platform Integrations, click on each accounting or financial platform your business uses. This is how Zac accesses your financial data to deliver reports.

  • Supported platforms: QuickBooks, Sage, Oracle NetSuite, Xero, Alteryx, Airtable, Power BI, Excel Online, and more
  • For each platform, choose how to grant access:
    • Share your existing login — provide your username and password
    • Create a separate advisor login — create a new login specifically for Lightning Ledgerz and share those credentials
  • Check the permission checkbox for each platform
  • Click "Grant Access Securely" — credentials are 256-bit encrypted
For Zac: You will receive an email to zprizant@lightningledgerz.com with the credentials immediately after the client submits. You can also view all credentials in the Admin Dashboard under the "Platforms" tab.
5

Upload Financial Documents

RECOMMENDED — SPEEDS UP DELIVERY
Client does this

In Step 3: Upload Files, drag and drop or browse to upload any financial documents for review.

Recommended documents to upload:

  • □ Prior year income statements (P&L)
  • □ Balance sheets (current + prior periods)
  • □ Cash flow statements
  • □ Existing budgets or forecasts
  • □ CapEx schedules
  • □ Debt schedules / loan summaries
  • □ Sources and uses of funds
  • □ Any existing reports or dashboards
6

Choose Your Plan & Start Billing

COMPLETE BEFORE TRIAL ENDS
Zac + Client

The free 30-day trial gives full access to the portal. A countdown timer in your dashboard shows how many days remain. At day 30, the portal locks and a plan is required to continue.

  • Basic — $799/month: Monthly reporting, budget vs. actual, email support
  • Gold — $1,299/month: Everything in Basic + weekly strategy calls, KPI dashboards, advanced modeling
  • Diamond — $3,999/month: Everything in Gold + fractional CFO, board presentations, direct phone access

To upgrade, click the "⬆ Upgrade" button in your dashboard header at any time, or visit the Pricing section on the website.

Promo codes: If Zac gave you a promotional code, enter it in the promo code field on the Pricing page before selecting your plan.
7

Receive Your Reports

ZAC ACTION
Zac does this

Once onboarded, Zac will upload completed reports and deliverables directly to your portal under Step 4: My Reports. You can scroll through your reports, search by title or date, and open any report directly from the portal.

If you have a live financial dashboard connected (Gold/Diamond clients), a "Live Financial Dashboard" button will appear at the top of your reports section.

Turnaround times: Basic — 5 business days for monthly reports. Gold — 2–3 business days. Diamond — next business day or same day for urgent items.
